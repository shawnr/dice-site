Qualifed for Federal Student Aid and Veterans Benefits

All NCS programs are fully qualified for Federal Student Aid and Veterans Benefits. You can use federal financial aid dollars to cover the costs of completing our degree and certificate programs. The process for obtaining federal aid for the Web Development Certificate is the same as when applying to any other University or degree program:

  1. Fill in your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
  2. Be sure to note "Seattle University" as a school you will be attending
  3. Submit your FAFSA
  4. Fill in an application to the Digital Cultures B.A. Degree Completion program and be sure to include your Social Security Number where requested

All NCS programs are eligible for Veterans Education Benefits.

You also need to send us your transcripts to complete the application process. At any point in time, feel free to email us at ncs_admissions@seattleu.edu or call at 206-296-6943 to talk through any issues, questions or concerns you have about financial aid.
